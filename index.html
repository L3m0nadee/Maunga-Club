Maunga Club

Valley Ski Area | New Zealand

Carve your own path to freedom

Explore the thrill of the slopes! The mountain offers 550 hectares of skiing/snowboarding and the longest ski/snowboard season in New Zealand.

Nestled amidst towering peaks and breathtaking alpine landscapes, our ski resort offers an unparalleled escape into the realm of winter wonder. With a seamless blend of adventure and comfort, our resort stands as a sanctuary for enthusiasts and novices alike.

All three chalets are on the mountain, so you can ski to and from the chalets.
